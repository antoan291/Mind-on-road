# Product Discovery

## Workflow

1. Read `AGENTS.md`, `docs/README.md`, and `agent/product/README.md`.
2. Identify the relevant product docs under `docs/product/` and related architecture docs.
3. Convert the request into explicit user roles, workflows, business rules, states, and acceptance criteria.
4. Identify missing decisions, contradictory requirements, regulatory/operational caveats, and out-of-scope assumptions.
5. Hand back a concise implementation-ready brief with:
   - problem statement;
   - user roles and flow;
   - business rules and edge cases;
   - acceptance criteria;
   - open questions;
   - impacted modules/docs.

If the business rule can be verified from repo docs, do not guess.
